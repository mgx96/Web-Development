Malek's All Time Favourite Movies

My Top 3 Movies

Top Gun: Maverick

As an aviation nerd, this movie was an absoulte banger!! It captures the feel of being inside the cockpit of a real fighter jet and very realistically conveys the different flying tactics and manuevers

Purple Hearts

In the midst of the politically charged division in the U.S between republicans and democrats, this movie depicts that we, as americans, can co-exist and live in harmony despite our political differences. I beleive we need more of these movies to spread postivity and ease the tension between people on both sides of the aisle

Any Veteran Movies

This might not qualify as a legitmate answer but it's true. The movies that shed some light on the sacrifices that our veterans have made in order to protect our nation are ones that I deeply admire, some more than others, but I geniunely beleive that we are unaware of the horrors these brave men and women go through to protect and serve their fellow Americans
